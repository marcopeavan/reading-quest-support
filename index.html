How to Use ReadingQuest

Hear a letter

Tap a letter box to hear it pronounced out loud.

Blend words

Drag your finger across a word to hear it blended as a full sound.

Practice reading

Use the app daily to build confidence with reading and pronunciation.

Spelling Quest

Spelling Quest is a game where you hear a word and choose the correct letters to spell it.

Check your answer

Tap the green checkmark to submit your spelling.

Incorrect letters

Wrong letters will wiggle in red. Tap them to replace with the correct letters.

Hints

Tap the orange question mark if you need a hint.

Hear the word again

Tap the blue speaker icon to replay the word.

Library

Add and save your own custom stories in your personal library.

FAQ

How do I track my reading?

Your progress is automatically saved as you log your sessions.

Currently, data is stored locally on your device.
